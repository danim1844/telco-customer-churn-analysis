# Telco Customer Churn Analysis
### A Data Analytics Case Study | SQL • Tableau • Excel

---

## Project Overview
This case study analyzes customer churn patterns for a mid-size telecom company 
to identify key factors driving cancellations, determine which customer segments 
are at highest risk, and provide data-driven retention recommendations.

**Business Question:** Which customers are most likely to churn, and what should 
the business do to retain them?

## Dataset
- **Source:** IBM Telco Customer Churn — Kaggle.com
- **Records:** 7,043 customers
- **Variables:** 33 columns
- **License:** Public, free to use

---

## Key Findings
- **26.54%** overall churn rate — 1 in 4 customers leaving
- **Month-to-month** customers churn at 42.71% vs just 2.83% for two-year contracts
- **New customers (0-12 months)** churn at 47.44% — the highest risk window
- **Attitude of support staff** is the #1 individual churn reason (10.27%)
- **Competition** drives ~43% of all churn combined
- A new month-to-month customer paying **$66+/month** has a **64-75% chance of churning**

## Top Recommendations
1. Launch an annual contract incentive program (10-15% discount)
2. Build a first 90-day retention program for new customers
3. Invest in customer service training immediately
4. Develop a competitive response strategy
5. Implement a churn prediction alert system using existing Churn Score data
